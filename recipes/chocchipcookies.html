Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookies

This delicious recipe creates big ooey gooey chewy chocolate chip cookies. Perfect for dunking in a glass of cold milk.

Ingredients

  • 10 oz All Purpose Flour
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 12 TBSP Butter, melted
  • 7 oz Brown Sugar
  • 3.5 oz White Sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 12 oz chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. First, whisk all of the dry ingredients in a medium bowl
  3. Then, melt the butter and whisk vigorously with both sugars until lighter in color.
  4. Next, whisk the egg and egg yolk until thoroughly combined
  5. Finally, stir in the dry ingredients and the chocolate chips. Mix until no dry ingredients remain.
  6. With a large cookie scoop or a 1/4 cup measuring cup, portion out cookie batter so evenly spaced on baking sheets.
  7. Bake until lightly golden brown, about 10-12 minutes.
