Cheddar and Chive Quiche

This cheesy quiche is easy to make and sure to please!

Ingredients

  • Premade piecrust
  • 5 eggs
  • 2 cups half and half
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 4 oz grated cheddar cheese
  • 3 TBL finely chopped chives

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Line 9-inch pie plate with pie crust. Fill with pie weights and par bake for until lightly golden, about 20 mintues.
  3. Whisk eggs, half and half, and salt together until homogeonous
  4. Sprinkle cheese chives into warm or cooled pie crust
  5. Pour egg mixture into pie crust
  6. Bake until center of quiche reaches 160 degrees, about 50-60 minutes
